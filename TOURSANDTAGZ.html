About Us

Founded in 2023, Tours and Tagz Shuttle Services has quickly established itself as a premier transportation provider in Gqeberha and the Eastern Cape region.

Our business model is centered around providing reliable, safe, and punctual shuttle services that our clients can depend on. We specialize in transportation solutions for ESL, hospitality, and nursing professionals, offering both one-time services and convenient monthly subscriptions.

At Tours and Tagz, safety is our top priority. All our vehicles undergo regular maintenance checks, and our drivers are professionally trained with excellent knowledge of local routes. We understand that being on time is not just a courtesy—it's a commitment we make to every client.

We pride ourselves on being your trusted partner to get you home safe, every time. Whether you're commuting to work, heading to the airport, or need transportation for a special event, you can count on Tours and Tagz Shuttle Services to deliver exceptional service with your safety and comfort as our primary focus.
